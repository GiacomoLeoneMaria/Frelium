# Privacy Policy — Frelium

**Last updated:** March 11, 2026

---

## 1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Frelium ("the App", "we", "our") handles your information. Frelium is developed and maintained by Giacomo Cavallini ("Developer", "I"), an individual developer based in Italy.

By downloading or using Frelium, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

---

## 2. Summary

**Frelium does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data.**

All data you enter in the App (transactions, clients, budgets, photos, settings) is stored exclusively on your device and never sent to any server, cloud service, or third party.

---

## 3. Data We Do NOT Collect

Frelium does not collect:

- Personal identifiers (name, email, phone number, IP address)
- Location data
- Device identifiers or advertising IDs
- Usage analytics or crash reports
- Financial data (all figures remain on your device)
- Biometric data

There are no accounts, no login, no registration.

---

## 4. Data Stored Locally on Your Device

The App stores the following data **exclusively on your device**:

| Data | Purpose | Where stored |
|---|---|---|
| Transactions (income & expenses) | Core app functionality | SQLite database (local) |
| Client information (name, color, hourly rate) | Core app functionality | SQLite database (local) |
| Client profile photos | Visual identification of clients | Device file system |
| Budget targets and currency settings | User preferences | AsyncStorage (local) |

This data is entirely under your control. You can export, import, or permanently delete it at any time from within the App.

---

## 5. Device Permissions

Frelium may request the following device permissions:

### Camera & Photo Library

- **Purpose:** To let you set a profile photo for clients
- **When:** Only when you explicitly tap "Add Photo" in the client form
- **Stored:** Photos are saved locally on your device only
- **Shared:** Never — photos are never uploaded or transmitted

You can revoke these permissions at any time from your device settings. The App will continue to function without them.

### Storage (Android only)

- **Purpose:** Required by the operating system to read/write backup files during export and import
- **Shared:** Never

- [Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)

## 8. Children's Privacy

Frelium is not directed at children under the age of 13 (or 16 in the EU/EEA as per GDPR). We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children. Since no data is collected at all, the App poses no known privacy risk to minors.

---

## 9. Your Rights Under GDPR (EU/EEA Users)

Because Frelium does not collect or process any personal data on external servers, the Developer does not act as a data controller under the GDPR in the traditional sense. You are the sole controller of all data stored on your own device.

Your rights with respect to your own data:

- **Right to access:** Your data is directly accessible within the App at all times
- **Right to deletion:** You can delete individual records or clear all data from Settings
- **Right to portability:** You can export all your data as a JSON file from Settings
- **Right to rectification:** You can edit any record at any time within the App

For any privacy-related questions, contact: [giacomoleonemariadev@gmail.com](mailto:giacomoleonemariadev@gmail.com)

---

## 10. Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device, security depends on your device's own protections (screen lock, encryption, etc.). We recommend:

- Keeping your device OS up to date
- Using a screen lock
- Making regular backups using the App's export feature

The Developer has no access to your data and cannot recover it if your device is lost or reset.

---

## 11. Data Retention

You control how long your data is kept. Data persists on your device until you:

- Manually delete records within the App
- Use the "Clear All Data" function in Settings
- Uninstall the App (which removes all local data)
